ONU GOODNESS AMADI

Goody's Nigerian made Ogbono Soup

Ingredients:

  • Ogbono seeds (ground)
  • Fish (catfish or tilapia)
  • Goat meat
  • Cow meat (beef)
  • Sliced Kpomo (cow skin)
  • Stock fish (dried cod)
  • Snail (cleaned)
  • Prawn
  • Periwinkle (optional)
  • Palm oil
  • Crayfish (ground)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Uziza leaves (optional)
  • Water

Cooking Steps:

  1. Boil the goat meat, cow meat, and kpomo with seasoning cubes and salt until tender. Set aside the broth.
  2. Soak the stock fish and snail in hot water, then add them to the pot and cook until soft.
  3. In a separate pot, heat palm oil, then add ground ogbono seeds and stir continuously until the mixture dissolves and begins to thicken.
  4. Add the meat broth to the dissolved ogbono mixture while stirring. Let it cook for about 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in the boiled meats (goat meat, cow meat, kpomo), stock fish, snail, prawn, and fish. Cook for another 5-7 minutes.
  6. Adjust seasoning by adding crayfish, seasoning cubes, and salt. If using uziza leaves, add in the final few minutes.
  7. Allow the soup to simmer for 5 minutes to ensure all flavors are combined, then serve with your preferred swallow (fufu, pounded yam, or eba).
